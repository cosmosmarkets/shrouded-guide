SHROUDED DEVELOPMENT

New Developer Guide

Welcome to Shrouded Development, a professional Roblox development agency built to connect skilled developers with serious, paid client work.

If you are reading this, congratulations. You have been selected to join our developer team. Please read this guide carefully before taking commissions.

1. Who We Are

Shrouded Development helps talented Roblox developers find reliable, high paying work without constantly searching for clients on their own.

We match clients with developers across roles such as:

• Modellers

• Builders

• Scripters

• Project Managers

• UI and UX Designers

• VFX Artists

• SFX Artists

• Animators

Our goal is to make commissions smoother, more organized, and more professional for both developers and clients.

2. What We Do

Shrouded Development helps by:

• Connecting developers with paid Roblox commissions

• Matching talent with suitable client projects

• Providing access to better paying opportunities

• Helping organize communication between clients, agents, and developers

• Managing tickets professionally

• Taking a small agency cut from completed work

This allows developers to focus more on creating, while Shrouded helps with finding and organizing work.

3. Commission Details

Commissions usually start at a minimum of $100 USD or 30,000 Robux, though prices can vary depending on the role, workload, deadline, and project complexity.

Some jobs may be lower or higher depending on what is required. For example, a small UI task will not be priced the same as a full scripting system, large build, animation pack, or project management role.

4. Agency Cut

Shrouded takes an agency cut from completed commissions so we can continue bringing in clients, managing tickets, and supporting developers.

New developers start with a 35% agency cut.

Your cut improves as you complete more commissions and demonstrate reliability. The tiers are as follows:

65% — Junior Developer

This is considered the starting cut for new developers, and a probation period.

70% — Developer

Granted after completing $350 worth of commissions successfully. You also unlock higher tier commissions, and will be prioritized for these over Junior Developers.

75% — Senior Developer

Granted after completing $2,000 worth of commissions successfully, and at least 5 separate commissions. Senior Developers also get given the ability to handle their own tickets for the most part, if they wish to.

80%+ — Partner

Reserved for long term, trusted developers. These are developers with custom deals between Shrouded and the developer.

Please keep in mind that ranks may be taken away due to inactivity, lack of cooperation, or any reason we deem appropriate. Each tier requires better communication, ability to meet deadlines, and consistent quality matching with that same tier. We may deny a tier up at any time — you’ll be given a reason as to why.

A few of the following reasons may be common:

• Missing deadlines repeatedly

• Ghosting projects

• Poor communication

• Low quality work

• Causing issues with clients

• Breaking any of the main rules

• Long inactivity

• Making the process harder than it has to be

5. How Matching Works

When a client opens a commission ticket, Shrouded reviews the request and matches it with suitable developers.

Before accepting any commission, make sure you:

• Read the entire ticket

• Understand exactly what the client wants

• Have the skill required for the work

• Have enough time to complete it properly

• Can communicate professionally

• Are confident you can deliver the final product

If you are unsure about a ticket, ask an agent, recruiter, or project manager before speaking in it.

6. Ticket Rules

Client tickets are professional spaces. Your behavior reflects both you and Shrouded.

Rules:

• Be professional in every client ticket

• Read the full ticket before speaking

• Only speak if you are seriously interested in taking the commission

• Do not claim work you cannot complete

• Do not leave the ticket after being matched with a client

• Do not ghost clients

• Give updates if you need time away

• Keep concerns out of client tickets

• DM staff or use the correct developer channel instead

Poor ticket behavior can damage client trust and may result in punishments or removal from commissions.

7. Communication Expectations

Good communication is required.

You are expected to:

• Respond in a reasonable time

• Be clear about your availability

• Give progress updates

• Warn staff or clients about delays

• Ask questions when requirements are unclear

• Stay respectful and professional

We understand that developers have school, work, and personal lives. However, Shrouded is still a business, and clients expect reliability.

8. Portfolios

Developers will be asked to send portfolios or examples of previous work. This helps us match you with the right clients.

Your portfolio should show your best relevant work, such as builds, models, scripts, systems, UI, animations, VFX, SFX, or completed Roblox projects.

Do not claim work that is not yours.

9. Activity Checks

Shrouded may run activity checks to see which developers are still active and interested in work.

If an activity check is posted, respond within the required time. Some checks may allow 48 hours, while others may allow longer.

Failure to respond may result in being marked inactive or removed from the developer roster.

10. Payments

Commissions may be paid through:

• USD

• Robux

• USD DevEx related payments

If you accept Robux, you will need to join the official Shrouded Development Roblox group.

Official Roblox Group: Open Roblox Group

If you only accept USD or DevEx related payments, let staff know.

11. Safety

Only trust official Shrouded staff, agents, recruiters, and heads for agency related commissions.

If someone outside the agency contacts you claiming to represent Shrouded, confirm with staff before replying.

12. Final Reminder

As a Shrouded developer, you represent both yourself and the agency.

Before taking any commission, remember:

Read the ticket. Understand the work. Communicate clearly. Stay professional. Deliver quality.

Shrouded Development is here to help skilled Roblox developers connect with serious clients, stronger commissions, and better opportunities.
